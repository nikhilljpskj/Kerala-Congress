PJ Joseph

P. J. Joseph, is a prominent Indian politician hailing from Kerala, representing the Kerala Congress. His political journey began in 1968 when he joined the Kerala Congress. Over the years, he has made significant contributions to the state's governance, serving as a Member of the Legislative Assembly from Thodupuzha Assembly Constituency in Kerala on ten occasions.

P. J. Joseph has held various ministerial positions, including Home Minister in 1978 and Revenue Minister from 1981 to 1987. He also served as the Education Minister from 1996 to 2001 and briefly as the PWD Minister in 2006 and 2010. Known for his dedication to public service, Joseph's political career has seen him navigate through alliances and party mergers, including the formation of Kerala Congress (Joseph). With a wealth of experience, P. J. Joseph continues to play a pivotal role in Kerala politics, now serving as the Chairman of Kerala Congress. His commitment to his constituents and his party's values makes him a respected leader in the region.
